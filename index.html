Questions

The honest FAQ

Do you file my taxes?
No. We prepare a verified, tax-ready package; you or your CPA file. We'd rather be the engine your accountant trusts than pretend to be one.
What if a number is wrong?
We re-close the month free and refund that month's fee. Every miss is logged and root-caused. To be plain about the limits: the guarantee is redo + refund — it isn't penalty insurance, which is exactly why the deliverable is a verified package your CPA signs off on, not a "trust us" number.
Do you handle sales tax / VAT?
Not in the pilot. We do your income-and-expense books and the tax-ready package; sales-tax registration and filings are out of scope — but because we see your revenue by state and country, we'll flag when you look like you're approaching a threshold worth asking a professional about.
Can you touch my money?
Never. Read-only connections. The agent prepares; only you act.
I'm not on Stripe — can I join?
The pilot is Stripe-first because it lets us verify revenue to the cent. Bank-only support follows right after.
